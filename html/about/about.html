ABOUT ISKRA

We believe the future of gaming is on the blockchain. Creating exciting opportunities for players, developers and investors, Iskra aims to break ground in Web3 gaming and lead the way into a new generation of blockchain entertainment.

OUR MISSION

Iskra aims to be more than just a part of the Web3 future — we aim to mold it. By creating a forward-thinking dedicated home for Web3 gaming and giving every user a stake in our success, we will become the leading platform for blockchain gaming, partnering with our entire community to build new opportunities for games together.

Celebrate Community

We bring individuals and organizations together to celebrate each other’s wins with a shared stake in the platform’s success.

Open Doors

Champion Games
