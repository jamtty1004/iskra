Recovery phrase

As the master key of your wallet and funds, the Recovery Phrase consist of 12 words.
Using the Recovery Phrase allow you to easily back up and restore your wallet without knowing the wallet password.

Never disclose your backup phrases. Anyone with these phrases can own your wallet assets forever.
Write these phrases down on a note and secure it in a safe place.
For enhanced security, write down these backup phrases and store it safely on an encrypted external hard drive or storage.
