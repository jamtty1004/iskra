Pioneer NFT Purchase

Important Notice Please read these terms carefully before purchasing Pioneer NFT.

About Pioneer NFT Pioneer NFT serves as a license to operate Iskra Nodes in the future.

Qualifications - You must sign up as a member and sign in to purchase NFTs. - OTP registration and verification are required after signing in.

Means of Purchase - You can only purchase with KLAY as of now. - Additional KLAY is required for transaction fee. - Have enough KLAY in your wallet.

Price of Pioneer NFT - The final payment amount may vary depending on the order of purchase and the price of purchase unit.
- The sooner you buy, the cheaper the price.

Purchase process - Purchasing Pioneer NFT is available in one stepper from verification to staking.
- The stepper consists of the following steps.

step 1. OTP verification
step 2. Select currency to purchase
step 3. Confirm the purchase order
step 4. Purchase
step 5. Check confirmed transaction
step 6. Select Pioneer NFT to stake
step 7. Confirm the stake order
step 8. Stake
step 9. Check confirmed transaction
step 10. View purchased Pioneer NFT in your Iskra Wallet

Compensation Conditions - You are not eligible for Pioneer NFT rewards only with a purchase.
- You must stake the purchased Pioneer NFT to receive rewards after purchase.
- You can check the reward through your Iskra wallet after staking.
- The ISK received as a reward is paid in a locked state. ISK rewards will be unlocked in sequence from the point when 5,000 Pioneer NFTs are sold.
- The ISK received as a reward is calculated in seconds, and is settled and paid in real time.

Exchange and Trade - Once purchased, Pioneer NFTs cannot be exchanged.
- Purchased Pioneer NFTs cannot be traded.
- Pioneer NFT trading among Explorers will be available in future milestones.

Refunds - Pioneer NFTs are non-refundable.

Unstaking - Unstaking is not available.
