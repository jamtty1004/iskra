Purchase Pioneer NFT

Important Notice
Please read these terms carefully before purchasing Pioneer NFT.

Purchase Eligibility
- Membership registration and sign-in are required to purchase NFT.
- OTP registration and authentication are required to proceed.

Payment Method
- Currently, you can only pay with KLAY and ISK.
- KLAY is required to pay the transaction fee.
- Prepare enough KLAY or ISK in your ISKRA wallet.

Price of Pioneer NFT
- The final payment amount may vary depending on the order of purchase and the price of the payment method.
- The sooner you buy, the cheaper the price.

Purchasing process
- You can purchase Pioneer NFT in one stepper from authentication to purchase and staking.
- The stepper consists of the following steps.

step 1. OTP authentication
step 2. Select payment method (KLAY or ISK)
step 3. Confirm your purchase
Step 4. Check out
step 5. Check the purchase history
step 6. Select Pioneer NFT to stake
step 7. Staking
step 8. Check the staking history
step 9. Check in Wallet

How to get rewards
- You cannot receive ISK rewards just by making a order or purchasing.
- After purchase, you must stake the purchased Pioneer NFT to receive rewards.
- After staking, you can view reward history through the wallet.
- The rewarded ISKs are kept in a locked state. ISK rewards will be unlocked sequentially from the time when 5,000 of Pioneer NFTs are sold.
- ISK reward is calculated in seconds and is paid daily.


- For the time being, you will not be able to trade, exchange, or sell the Pioneer NFT you purchased


- Refunds can be made through the Help Center within 7 days of purchase.
- After 7 days, Pioneer NFTs are non-refundable.
- Staked Pioneer NFTs are non-refundable.
- In case of refund, a full refund will be made to the wallet by the payment method you chose.
- It takes up to 2-4 working days to process the refund.


- You need to stake Pioneer NFT until ISKRA 2.0 upgrade is complete.
- After upgrade, unstaking and withdrawals of Pioneer NFT are enabled by ISKRA network.
- Please note that staked Pioneer NFT cannot currently be unstaked.
